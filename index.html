5th Annual Turkey Sausage Cutting Competition

History

Once apon a time, the Nippert household had a spare turkey sausage in their fridge, and so they took it out and cut a thin slice. They then thought that they could make this into a competition; And thus, the Annual Turkey Sausage Cutting Competition was born.

Rules

Take turns cutting the sausage as thin as possible with at least 80% of the sausage in tact The thinnest slice after 3 rounds is declared winner. Error is considered +- 0.2mm. If there is a tie, a sudden death round occurs where each contestant must cut yet another slice in a fierce head to head scenario.
